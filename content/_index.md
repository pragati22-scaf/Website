## What is Pragati?

"Pragati 2K22, is the annual cultural and academic festival also known as Sree Chitra Arts Fest (SCAF), organised by the students of Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences & Technology (SCTIMST), Trivandrum for the year 2022. It is aimed at building a sense of family within the student community."
