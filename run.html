What is Pulse of Chitra?

“The Pulse of Chitra, or ‘Student Fest of Chitra’, is an annual cultural and academic festival organised by the students of Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences & Technology (SCTIMST), Trivandrum. It is aimed at building a sense of family within the student community.”
